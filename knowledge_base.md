# Foliox AI — Complete Knowledge Base

## Executive Overview

### What is Foliox AI?
Foliox AI is an AI-driven logistics and dispatch automation platform specializing in fuel distribution, wholesale operations, chemical dispatching, and multi-site delivery networks. The platform transforms logistics operations from reactive, manual coordination to predictive, intelligent automation.

### Mission
To eliminate repetitive manual work in logistics operations, anticipate operational risks before they impact business, and enable dispatch teams to focus on strategic decisions rather than routine tasks.

### Core Value Proposition
- **Embedded Intelligence**: AI that works within existing workflows, not alongside them
- **Predictive Operations**: Forecasting problems before they occur, not just responding to them
- **Automated Compliance**: Reducing regulatory risk through continuous monitoring and reporting
- **Market Intelligence**: Real-time terminal pricing and supply visibility
- **Connected Ecosystem**: Unified platform linking field operations, dispatch, and management

### Target Market
Companies operating complex, multi-site logistics environments where inventory forecasting, compliance tracking, and real-time dispatch adjustments are mission-critical:
- Fuel distribution networks
- Chemical delivery operations
- Multi-site retail fuel logistics
- Fleet dispatch operations
- Wholesale fuel and petroleum operations

## Platform Architecture

### Design Philosophy
Foliox AI is architected as a **middleware intelligence layer** that sits between existing operational systems and end users. Rather than replacing dispatch systems, it enhances them with AI capabilities.

### Key Architectural Principles
1. **Embedded Integration**: Fits into existing workflows without forcing process redesign
2. **Real-time Processing**: Continuous data ingestion and instant recommendation generation
3. **Human-AI Collaboration**: AI suggestions with dispatcher oversight and control
4. **Scalable Intelligence**: Built on cloud infrastructure with modular components

### System Components

#### AI Engine
- AIDA (AI Dispatch Assistant) for operational automation
- Sentinel Agent for terminal intelligence
- Predictive analytics for demand forecasting
- Optimization algorithms for dispatch planning

#### Business Logic Layer
- 120+ embedded automation rules
- Compliance checking and validation
- Safety constraint enforcement
- Priority scoring and routing logic

## AIDA: AI Dispatch Assistant

AIDA is Foliox's central AI teammate, designed not as a standalone chatbot but as an embedded operational assistant that automates tasks within existing dispatcher workflows.

### Core Functions
- **Operational Prediction**: Forecasts demand, identifies shortages, predicts carrier availability, anticipates weather disruptions
- **Communication Automation**: Crafts emails/SMS, manages follow-ups, handles status updates, automates escalations
- **Proactive Intelligence**: Real-time alerts for compliance/supply/safety, contextual recommendations, optimization insights
- **Workflow Integration**: Connects with existing dispatch systems, ERP, WMS, TMS

### Interaction Model
1. **Autonomous Execution**: Routine tasks without human intervention
2. **Recommendation Mode**: Suggestions for dispatcher approval
3. **Alert Mode**: Flags exceptions requiring human judgment

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Dispatch Workflow Automation
Eliminates repetitive coordination tasks. Automated messaging, follow-up management, status coordination, exception handling. Reduces "fires to put out" during each shift.

### 2. Inventory Intelligence & Forecasting
Continuous monitoring with predictive analytics. **< 1% stock-out rate** in production. **2-5% working capital savings**. Velocity-based priority scoring for site replenishment.

### 3. Bill of Lading (BOL) Reconciliation
Automates document matching - reduces reconciliation from **20+ hours/week to < 2 hours/week**. Document ingestion, data extraction, matching logic, exception flagging.

### 4. Driver Performance & Compliance Tracking
HOS monitoring, certifications, safety records, vehicle compliance. Covers DOT, Hazmat, state-specific regulations.

### 5. Automated Compliance Reporting
Aggregates sensor, testing, and alarm data. Centralized dashboards, audit-ready reports, automatic flagging.

### 6. Terminal Intelligence: Sentinel Agent
Real-time terminal fuel prices and supply monitoring. Price tracking, trend analysis, arbitrage identification, bulk purchase timing.

### 7. Dynamic Dispatch Optimization
Adjusts dispatch plans in real-time. Greedy algorithms with LLM enhancement. Physics-based constraints (gasoline 0.75 lb/gal, diesel 0.85 lb/gal, DOT 80,000 lb limit). Recommendation types: better site selection, fuel type swaps, split load opportunities.

### 8. Alerts & Alarms System
Compliance, supply, safety, and operational alerts with contextual prioritization and escalation logic.

## AI Agent Architecture

### Dual-Agent Security Model
Two-agent architecture for enterprise security:
- **Intake Agent**: Customer-facing, extracts structured requests, cannot execute operations
- **Dispatch Agent**: Internal execution, requires explicit approval for all actions
- Container isolation, separate credentials, approval workflow guardrails
